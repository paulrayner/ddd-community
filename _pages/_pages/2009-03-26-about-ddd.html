Vladimir Gitlevich is a software architect in San Francisco. He has been developing software for over two decades. For several years he was a part of Domain Language, where he consulted on a number of projects as a design mentor. Vladimir is the site's editor and webmaster. He's also the founder of DDD-NYC SIG.
