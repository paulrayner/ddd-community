Enrollment is now open for the 4-day class, "DDD Hands-on Immersion", at
these locations and times:

  • London September 30-Oct 3
  • Stockholm October 7-10 (in Swedish)

This intensive course by Eric Evans brings together the practices that
give those object models a real impact on a project. Through lectures,
simulations, discussions and hands-on exercises, we cover

  • The model discovery process
  • Communication between software experts and domain experts
  • Building block patterns for effective domain models
  • Strategic design and applying domain models on large projects

This class is open to developers, analysts and technical leaders, as
well as development managers and other non-technical members of the
software development team.
