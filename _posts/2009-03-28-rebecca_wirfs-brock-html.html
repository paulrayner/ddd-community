Rebecca Wirfs-Brock is one of those people who did DDD long before it was called that. She kindly agreed to be interviewed by Vladimir Gitlevich on behalf of dddcommunity.org. In this interview, Rebecca talks about DDD in relation with responsibility-driven design and OO modeling, integration of DDD with technical frameworks such as Hibernate. She also gives advice on telling
design stories and explaining domain models.
