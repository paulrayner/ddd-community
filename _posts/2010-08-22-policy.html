This is a non-commercial site, but commercial events are an important part of the community's activity. Therefore, we have established the following guidelines to decide what events we will announce:
  • Must be primarily for education about DDD or discussion of it.
  • Must be scheduled and open to the public. (In other words, services being offered privately to clients do not qualify.)
  • Many such events are free, but a fee may be charged, as with a conference presentation or an open enrollment class.
