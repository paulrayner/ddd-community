Vaughn Vernon at QCon SF 2010 advocates using DDD’s strategic design patterns when integrating services in a RESTful SOA implementation, avoiding one of SOA’s pitfalls: focusing on services rather than the domain.

He shows how Context Mapping allows us to use SOA and DDD together, getting the best from each.
