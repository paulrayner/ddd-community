Daniel Gackle interviews Dan Bergh Johnsson on Domain-Driven Security, an approach to solving the problems of application security, like SQL injection and cross-site scripting, using core principals of DDD.

Read Dan's blog to learn more about Domain-Driven Security.

The interview was recorded in May of 2013 at the DDD Summit in Portland, ME.
