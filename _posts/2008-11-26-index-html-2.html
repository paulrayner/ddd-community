At the end of QCon San Francisco conference, right after the DDD track, we sat down with Eric Evans for an interview about the current state of the art in Domain-Driven Design.

We spoke about the evolution of Domain-Driven Design in the years since the book was published, its adoption across the globe, the community, the sample application, new DDD patterns and the shift in pattern importance, the future of DDD and more.
